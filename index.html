About Me

Brandon Wood is a RF and Avionics Engineer from Brooklyn, New York. After Studying at Cornell University, he worked for Second Order Effects where he helped to design several power and RF systems for space applications. Following this, Brandon moved to Raleigh where he worked with GaN on SiC power amplifiers at Wolfspeed. He has since returned to Cornell to complete his Master's and has been studying CMOS cryogenic LNA design.
